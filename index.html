About the Empire

The Honkan Empire is a sovereign organization founded upon principles of pride, national unity, and security. It operates as a hybrid system incorporating monarchic, democratic, militaristic, capitalistic, and stratocratic structures.

The Empire is led by its Supreme Grand Premier, Braedyn Mannion, alongside Grand Premier Jacob Leys and Premier Emerson Skipper. Together, they oversee governance, coordination, and strategic development within the Empire.

Vision Statement

“For a safer, more secure, and stronger community.”

Purpose

The Honkan Empire exists to foster unity, structure, and progress through organized leadership and collective participation. Members are encouraged to contribute to the growth and stability of the Empire.
